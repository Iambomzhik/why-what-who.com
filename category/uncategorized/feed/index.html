Our router is a fundamental part for the Internet connection to work correctly. It is what allows us to have a speed adequate, stability and avoid problems that cause connection failures. But you also have to keep in mind that not all routers are the same.

Over time, routers have significantly improved the quality of your connection, knowing how to support more connected devices, with higher transfer speeds and even more wireless coverage.

In this sense, and as the colleagues from RedesZonethe time may come when we have an older router model at home that may be giving us certain problems when connecting to the Internet or that, simply, we are curious to know if it would be possible to improve the connection using a latest router. At this point you have two possibilities: either you contact your operator to provide you with a new generation model, or choose to buy one on our own. But what do we have to value?

What to consider

In any case, it is important to appreciate that an old or obsolete router can cause problems, both for the Internet and for other uses. Therefore, we must take into account certain fundamental aspects to be able to make a comparison regarding their functions and thus understand when they are stopping working correctly.

With the same connection, if we use a quality router we can appreciate a great improvement in web browsing, but much more in WiFi coverage and the speed obtained, as well as noticing the difference when playing online.

In any case, it is important for you to acquire a new and good equipment that can meet the needs depending on what needed at home. Although the main recommendation is that you buy one that offers you guarantees of at least two years.

Slow down or low bandwidth issues

A very important factor is related to the uses of the Internet in the domestic environment and the number of users of a single connection. If there are several of us at home, or we have a multitude of terminals associated with a network, sometimes slowdown issues very significant. It is when it is said that the bandwidth of our router is short and our slow internet. In these cases, it may be necessary to purchase another device.

Keep in mind that we have more and more devices connected to the network. We especially talk about what is known as the Internet of Things. This will mean that they need to have adequate bandwidth. We must avoid problems and that the router cannot offer what the devices require.

Is it WiFi 6 compatible?

In this direction, a key approach also emerges: WiFi 6: if our router is not compatible with this standard, that will make it even more evident that we must get a new one.

Thus, in the technical file of the device, they should include either the official name or the name of the standard. Sometimes it will just say something like “Wifi 802.11ax” or, more succinctly, “Wi-Fi b, g, n, ac, ax.” Each letter refers to a WiFi standard. What interests us isx, equivalent to Wi-Fi 6.

Poor results in speed tests

Another very important problem that can occur with our router and its Internet connection can be detected through speed tests, which will test the network frequency. We recommend that two tests be done: one of the wireless network and the other by connecting the Ethernet cable to a computer.

If the result of the connection with the cable is superior, it is necessary to do the test again. If the numbers are still different, the router may no longer be working as it should and it’s time to make that change.

number of ports

Starting from the basis that it is normal to have fiber, we must begin to make distinctions. If your router is capable of providing two networks, one 2.4 GHz and one 5 GHz, it falls into the category of modern routers; although if it is linked to the fiber, the better.

And there comes the number of ports that the device has. They typically have four Ethernet ports. Some have more and others may have just one or two, depending on the case. Generally with four ports it is enough for a house. For its part, in addition, the clear indication will be if it does not have a port GigabitEthernet, what it means is that it is a fairly old router.

]]> Know all the data on the hard drive or SSD with CrystalDiskInfo https://why-what-who.com/know-all-the-data-on-the-hard-drive-or-ssd-with-crystaldiskinfo/ Sun, 29 May 2022 14:51:53 +0000 https://why-what-who.com/know-all-the-data-on-the-hard-drive-or-ssd-with-crystaldiskinfo/ A hard drive can fail for various reasons, and one of the most important can be found in the excessive temperature to which we submit them when we work with the PC, and this is where an application like CrystalDiskInfo can be very useful, since it will offer us a good amount of data in an orderly and efficient way.

And we are talking about software that is as simple as it is powerful, and once executed, it will take care of offering us real time information of details as important as the brand, model, serial number, among other elements, and indicating the state in which it is found.

How does it work

Once we run the program, its main interface appears where we will find all kinds of detailed information. Among the information that the program shows us, it is worth highlighting:

  • Health condition: is responsible for making an average of all the values ​​and tells us what state the disk is in.
  • Temperature: from here it shows us the temperature of the disk, so to avoid problems it is convenient that it be as low as possible.
  • Device Overview: In this section we can see the brand, model, firmware, operating time and other general information about the HDD or SSD.
  • Individual SMART information: here, we can see the status of each of the points controlled by the SMART to know if, although the disk appears to be fine, there is a point where it could fail.

In the upper part we find several tabs, where it is convenient to highlight its section of Functionfrom where, we find the different functions that we can carry out with the program.

From here we have different options to determine how and how often the program data is refreshed. It also has the function of Graph from where we can see the behavior of the available memories. We can also hide serial numbersomething that can be very useful if we are going to share screenshots of the program.

Another interesting section is its Advanced Options from where we will be able to configure the temperature scale, the waiting time to start the program, carry out an advanced search for hard drives or hide the information that SMART offers us.

Is it worth using?

This software is responsible for providing us with a complete reading of all the SMART information of our PC, as well as the current temperature and its health status. It has a clear interface that makes it easy for us to find all the information we need. On the contrary, it is also worth mentioning that unless we have a certain knowledge of the information that it shows us, we will not be able to deduce much from it, since it does not have any help or explanation function for the data that it offers. That is why in order to take full advantage of it, it must be used mainly by Advanced users. In this case, you apply with an excellent free option.

Free download

CrystalDiskInfo is a free Software, so we can use it without having to pay a single euro, by simply downloading it from its official website. The program is compatible with Windows in its different versions up to Windows 11, as well as with 32-bit and 64-bit systems.

It has three download options since we can choose from different skins: Standard Edition, Shizuku Edition and Kurei Kei Edition. All the options are identical in terms of the software content, since it only modifies the aesthetic section. In each of the versions we will have up to four sections for downloading the program, either compressed or uncompressed, as well as installable or portable.

Of course we will recommend the wearable version, since it does not require installation and therefore does not modify the Windows registry. In addition, we can run it directly from any external storage device such as a hard drive, a flash drive, or even a memory card.

Other alternatives

If we are looking for a program with which we can find out the status of our hard drive or SSD, we can also opt for one of these alternatives to CrystalDiskInfo:

HD Tune

We are talking about a program with which we can carry out a visual analysis of our hard drive and check its internal behavior. It also scans your SMART data as well as your bad sectors. In addition, it is also used to measure the temperature of the HDD or SSD and its hours of use. We can download HD Tune from the developer’s website.

DiskCheckup

It is a program with which we are going to be able to carry out basic diagnostic work on the state of the storage units that we have on our computer, being able to monitor the SMART data, and even configure alarms so that the program notifies us in the event that the hard drive reaches a high temperature. If we want to try DiskCheckup, we can download it from its main web page.

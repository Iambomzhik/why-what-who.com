Technical characteristics, price and availability

ByAd007

May 28, 2022

The best and the worst

We have one of the strengths of the Xiaomi 12 Pro just by seeing the phone. AN absolutely premium design, a high-quality mobile that not only promises us power and good photo but it gives us elegance and high quality materials… It also highlights a brilliant screen of a very high range.

And not only does it stand out in design, it also does so with an outstanding camera team. 50 triple camera megapixels that make the Xiaomi 12 Pro one of the best quality mobile phones in photo and video.

Among the drawbacks is MIUI, a customization layer that not all users like and that can be somewhat more annoying than other Xiaomi layers. In addition, the battery is the main complaint of all users and it is also one of the failures of this mobile premium: won’t last a full day of heavy use. In return, fast charging allows us to have it ready in a few minutes if we need it.

Price range

The Xiaomi 12 Pro is a premium mobile, a high-end mobile that we can find with a base price of 999.99 euros. Is available in two different versions of RAM albeit with the same internal storage. The 8 GB RAM version with 256 GB storage is priced at €999.99 and the 12 GB RAM version with 256 GB storage is priced at €1,099.99.

If we take a look at an Amazon price comparator such as CamelCamelCamel we will see that there is no price change in the phone since its launch and that It has always cost 999.99 euros in this online store. It is also the current price if we choose not to buy it from the official Xiaomi store.

In which operators can it be obtained

We can get the Xiaomi 12 Pro 5G with Vodafone. The phone came exclusively with this operator. We can get the phone in black color or in blue color and the model with 256 GB capacity and we can get it with portability, with a new number in the operator or if we are already Vodafone customers.

The spot price is 936 euros with portability or 1097 euros for a new number. If we choose to pay it in installments, we will have to pay from 32 euros per month to get the phone in 24 months with a new number and with an initial payment of 329 euros. We can also get it in installments with portability, choosing between 24 or 36 months and with an initial payment from 198 euros.

ADSLZone review

The Xiaomi 12 Pro is not a mobile phone for all pockets but it is a fully recommended smartphone if you are looking for high quality, if you want to take the best photos with a phone without missing a camera in your hands. In addition, it is also one of the best mobiles if we are looking for a totally premium design, if we want to have “the best” in our hands…

But it also has drawbacks and it is not an off-road phone for those who want to have a mobile whose battery lasts all day because its autonomy will make us always carry the charger with us… In addition, MIUI can be very annoying And with too many options, pure Android would always be a more recommended option to complete all the benefits of its design and its camera.

Frequent questions

What are the most frequent doubts that we can have before buying a Xiaomi 12 Pro? What are the things we should note about this Xiaomi mobile phone? Is it WiFi 6 compatible? And does it come with a cover?

  • Is it WiFi 6 compatible?

Yes, the Xiaomi 12 Pro is compatible with WiFI 6 networks and also supports bluetooth 5.2 in addition to being a phone compatible with 5G network technology.

Yes, the mobile phone includes a protective case, a USB Type C cable and an adapter, along with the quick start guide and the guarantee card of the mobile.

  • How long does it take to load?

The fast charging of the mobile phone allows us charge 100% in just 18 minutes if we choose the high performance mode. Or we can charge 100% in 24 minutes if we use the standard mode. In addition, it has wireless charging that allows us to fully charge without cables in just 42 minutes on the Xiaomi charger.

  • In what colors can we buy it?

The phone is available in three colors available in both 12GB RAM version and 8GB RAM version. We can get it in blue, gray or purple.

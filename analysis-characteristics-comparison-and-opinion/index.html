The best and the worst

Among the main advantages and benefits of this Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra we have its design and its screen. The phone has an outstanding construction and in our hands we notice that we have a phone of the highest range, of excellent quality. In addition, its screen has outstanding quality if we are looking for a good image, brightness…

The main drawback of the Samsung Galaxy S22 UltrA is that it is an outstanding phone but not suitable for all pockets because its price exceeds 1,200 euros. Also, there are other small inconveniences that can make it not for us as an autonomy that may not be prepared for intense use for a whole day and may fall short. Also, the performance is not the best compared to other phones in the same range or price range.

User opinion

If we take a look at the comments on Amazon of the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, we can see that the majority of opinions value the phone with five stars or four stars. A mobile in which the users of the online store have highlighted aspects comor the battery, the camera, the fast charge or the performance of the mobile.

In a comment by Daniel on March 8, 2022, he indicates:

«We have in front of us the best Samsung mobile to date, but honestly it seems to me an excessive price, since for €1359 we have a mobile similar to the previous ones with some small improvement, on top of that we can add that this year comes with the novelty that it does not come with a charger. We can say that luckily I got an offer in which the phone came with a charger, but the 25w one, much to be desired from this company. Currently, I think they should improve face recognition, since Apple completely eats up Samsung in this area. In addition, the fingerprint recognition leaves something to be desired, since when you put on a protector it does not work correctly, they should look for another area…”

Sergio also talks about a “bittersweet feeling” with the Samsung mobile in a comment in March 2022 on the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra website.

“I have bittersweet feelings, it goes very well and is a great high-end, but it has little things that do not leave me completely satisfied for the price of €1,350. the fingerprint is slow compared to other brands, if you put a protector on it, forget that you don’t have to apply your finger more than twice to be recognized. I notice a lack of coverage at times and on the internet it sometimes gets a bit stuck, having to close the window since does not let go forward or backwards. The screen is very good but the color of the icons of the applications themselves are not very bright, the truth is that it leaves me somewhat dissatisfied for the price paid but I have a couple of returns from other mobiles and I don’t know if Amazon might not like a new change . It’s been a while since I’m satisfied with the high-end prices for what they really give. It may be because of its exynos processor and that with the 8 gen 1 it goes much better, I don’t know, but it’s not something huge either, it’s a very expensive phone for what it claims to give and then it really gives. Other than that, highly recommended.”

Problems we can find

Although it may seem simple to increase the volume, we must bear in mind that, when doing so, we can find different problems. This is because the louder the volume, the more the sound tends to distort. And this can happen, on the one hand, due to the algorithms used by the programs to increase the sound, or, on the other hand, due to the limitations of our headphones or speakers.

Before resorting to this type of program, it is better to try to increase the volume in other ways. For example, by using Windows’ own volume control, or by physically turning up the volume of the speakers, in case they have an individual volume control.

If the sound is distorted, or heard badly, there is little more we can do. It may be that the quality is poor (and a low sound is used to hide it), or that our speakers cannot turn up the volume any more. In both cases, we have few ways to fix these problems. In the first case, the only solution would be to look for a new audio with higher quality that allows us to amplify it without losing quality, and in the second case, either connect an external amplifier, or buy other speakers with greater power to be able to reproduce these sounds better.

And it is that, if we abuse this functionality, we can distort the exit of that audio, damage speakers or headphones, or even harm ourselves.

Which is the best of all?

As we can see, there is a good variety of programs that allow us to control the volume of our computer in a much more precise way than what Windows itself offers us. In addition, each program has its own pros and cons, each of them being perfect for one task or another.

If what we are looking for is a kind of equalizer for Windows, then the first of the programs that we must try is FxSound. This program will allow us to adjust the sound of the PC according to a series of predefined modes, or adjust each one of the channels according to our tastes.

On the other hand, if what we are looking for is to be able to amplify the volume of our headphones or speakers above 100%, then the program that we must install on the computer is none other than Letasoft Sound Booster. Thanks to this software, we are not only going to recover the classic slider to adjust the volume of the PC, but we can even raise this volume above 100%, specifically up to 500%.

Finally, if what we are looking for is to control the sound of each of the programs that we have open on the computer, the software that we must install is EarTrumpet. This program is anchored in the system tray and, when you click on it, it shows us a list with all the PC applications that are generating sound, being able to raise or lower the sound of each of them individually.

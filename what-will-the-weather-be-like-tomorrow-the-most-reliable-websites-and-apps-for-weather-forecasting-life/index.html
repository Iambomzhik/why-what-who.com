What will the weather be like tomorrow? The most reliable websites and apps for weather forecasting | Life

ByAd007

May 15, 2022

In the middle of the year 2022, at the time of the metaverse, cryptocurrencies and private space travel, we can say that We have mastered the weather. Or at least, the way to protect ourselves from it.

If it’s cold, we bundle up and turn on the heating. If it’s hot, the air conditioning. If it rains, the umbrella. We travel in vehicles that protect us from all the elements, and most people work indoors. So, Why this obsession with knowing what the weather will be like tomorrow?

It is true that for people who work outdoors, farmers and other jobs, it is important know the weather tomorrow. But it doesn’t affect most of us too much.

Thinking of buying a penguin? We tell you how to choose a portable air conditioner so you can forget about being hot this summer.

Perhaps it is an ancestral echo of past times, when we did depend on the whims of the sky. Or the sensation of dominating Nature that comes from knowing that tomorrow it will rain, even though today there is not a cloud in the sky.

In any case, the reasons are irrelevant. you are here to meet the best websites and apps to find out what the weather will be like tomorrow, and we will provide them to you immediately.

Best websites and apps for weather forecast

best websites

Many people still check the weather from their laptop, or do not want to install an app just for that.

The fastest way to access Weather forecastIt is through any browser. These are the best weather websites.

The time is

It is one of the classic websites, which has been working in the field of weather forecasts for many years.

Offers daily forecast up to 14 days in advanceas well as monthly analysis, and weather alerts.

Different types of maps can be consulted: satellite, clouds, temperature, wind, pressureetc., and news about the weather, climate change, and much more.

It has a complete section with air quality data in the main towns.

Access the weather

weather.com

weather.com offers the weather forecast for the next 14 days in Spain and the rest of the world. is able to do an hourly forecastand also includes sunrise and sunset, lunar calendar, and other data.

incorporates rain, snow, temperature and wind weather maps, numerical prediction models and weather alerts for the entire country. It also provides the latest satellite and rain radar images.

Finally we have news, a magazine, and a forum.

meteosat.com

One of the most complete and reliable weather websites.

It has the hour by hour weather forecastand includes data such as temperature, wind, precipitation, humidity, clouds, pressure, dew pointand much more.

It also has Meteosat satellite images updated in real time and with animations. Includes infrared vision, water vapor, visible image, and much more.

With an alerts section, and even live cameras from the International Space Station.

Go to Meteosat.com

AEMET

The State Meteorological Agency It has its own website with weather forecasts. It shows a general prediction for all of Spain, and then a local one, with a search engine by municipalities.

The data included is very complete: state of the sky, temperatures, wind chill, humidity, winds, precipitation, etc.

It also boasts a complete section of alerts, news, and video predictions. One of the best websites to check the weather.

Access Aemet

AccuWeather

If what you are looking for is a world Weather Forecast, One of the most used pages is AccuWeather.

has a weather radar type Google Maps that covers the whole world, and where you can move freely. By “zooming in” you can access local forecasts for rain, snow, and ice.

It is also possible to search by location, and access data on hurricanes and extreme weather.

Access AccuWeather

Meteox

Meteox It is basically real-time rainfall radar.

Using satellite data updated in real time every 15 minutes, it shows you on the screen how the clouds move

There is also weather forecasts every 15 minutes, and long-term forecasts. A good website if what you are interested in is knowing if it is going to rain in the next few minutes.

Go to Meteox

meteoblue

meteoblue we like it because it offers the weather forecast in a very visual and easy to see way.

At a glance we get the maximum and minimum temperature, and if it is going to rain. A more detailed study shows us the wind, and the amount of precipitation. There is also data that other websites do not usually include, such as the hours of sunshine. The forecast is offered in hourly intervals.

We also like the Meteogram that mixes data such as temperatures, clouds and wind speed on a single map.

Access MeteoBlue

Best apps for iOS and Android

If you are one of those who is used to checking the weather forecast every day, while having breakfast or when you are going to leave home or work, the most practical thing is install an app.

Some of the websites mentioned above also have their own app. In order not to repeat ourselves, here we include different ones.

rain alarm

As its name suggests, this app specializes in record rain forecast in real time. It shows cloud movement almost instantly, and a color guide lets you know the intensity of precipitation.

Very useful for all outdoor activities such as biking, motorcycling, hiking, gardening, barbecues, picnics, dog walking, home repairs and much more.

You can receive notifications, and even vibrations and alarms on the mobile, when it’s about to rain in your location.

Download Rain Alarm on Google Play

Weather on iOS and Android

You don’t need to install anything to check the weather on your mobile.

Both Android itself and the system layer of many manufacturers have an integrated weather app.

In the case of Android there is a widget that lets you check the weather from your home screenor the main cover of the mobile.

By tapping on the widget you access the current forecast. It also includes a forecast of temperature and rain for the next 5 days.

iOS also has its own app, based on The Weather Channel. With its own widget and support for multiple cities.

Climate – Weather

One of the most complete weather forecast applications for mobile.

collect the detailed weather conditions of a month in the future. With a weather radar in real time.

Other very useful tools that it incorporates are the widget, and the integration in the clock.

Download Weather -Weather

Weather forecast

We like this app because at a glance you have all the important data on the screen: current temperature, minimum and maximum, rain, sunrise and sunset, wind, etc.

The forecasts are in real time, and with a view to 3 or 7 days. not missing weather maps, radar animations, graphs, and other information.

A really complete app that is worth trying.

Download Weather Forecast

Weather & Radar

Another of the most downloaded weather forecast apps, both on iOS and Android.

The application Weather & Radar he offers you 90-minute, hourly, daily, weekly and 14-day weather forecast in Spain and anywhere in the world.

Always have up-to-date data on temperature, rain, snow, wind, atmospheric pressure, relative humidity and probability of precipitation, as well as sunrise and sunset times, at your fingertips.

What you would never imagine that nature is capable of doing: spectacular phenomena that occur daily

The radar shows where the rain, hail or snow falls in real time. If there is a storm, risk of ice or strong storm, you will receive from Weather & Radar an alert on your mobile device in the form of a push notification.

This app also measures current pollen levels and the forecast for the next few days for any town in Spain. You can also see air quality levels.

Download Weather & Radar on Android
